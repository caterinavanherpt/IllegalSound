Welcome to

Illegal Sound
Entertainment Group

Owners Johnny Vivaqua and Marco Di Vincenzo have been in the industry for over 15 years, from bars to nightclubs to now wedding and corporate events Illegal Sound Entertainment Group has evolved bringing together professional entertainers to make your event one you always remember. Offering a wide variety of lighting, music and state of the art sound. Illegal Sound has and will always leave a great impression to last a life time.

Serving the GTA and the golden horseshoe we are proud to provide a mobile service to our clientele making the planning of your event as enjoyable as the night to come. For inquires or bookings please contact us.
